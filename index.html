The beautiful Island Madagascar

Madagascar is an island nation located off the southeastern coast of Africa in the Indian Ocean. It is the fourth largest island in the world, known for its unique biodiversity, with many species of plants and animals found nowhere else on Earth. The country has a rich cultural heritage, blending indigenous Malagasy traditions with influences from Africa, Asia, and Europe.
